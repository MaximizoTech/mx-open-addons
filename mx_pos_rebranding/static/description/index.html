Odoo POS Rebranding & Customization

A powerful all-in-one Point of Sale customization module by MaximizoTech. Fully white-label your POS interface, secure your refunds, and elevate your retail experience!

🌟 Key Features

🎨 Custom Theme & Branding

  • Custom POS Logo: Replace the Odoo logo with your brand.
  • Theme Color Picker: Easy HTML5 color pickers in config.
  • Smart Contrast: Text colors adjust automatically for readability.
  • Customer Display: Clean, branded secondary screens.

🛡️ Enhanced Security & UI Tools

  • Refund Protection: Password-protected refund access.

  • Idle Screen: Custom idle text and branded browser tabs.

🧾 Custom Receipt Templates

Choose from beautifully designed, built-in receipt layouts directly from your POS config settings.
